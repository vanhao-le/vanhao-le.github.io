I have been doing my Phd in Computer Vision field at LIFAT laboratory since Nov-2020. Before, I was a lecturer at Faculty of Information and Communication Technologies (FICT), Hong Duc University (HDU) since 2012. I received my M.Sc in Information System from VNU University of Engineering and Technology (UET) in 2016 and my B.Sc in Information Technology from HDU in 2011.

I am very interested in the domain of image processing, computer vision, and deep learning. In particular, I am working on the topic Partial Video Copy Detection (PVCD) as my PhD thesis. The PVCD goal is identifying and locating if one or more segments of a long testing video have been copied (transformed) from the reference videos dataset.
